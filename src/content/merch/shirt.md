Get a mouthful of Tassie with the Dave's Eats Hobart tee — inspired by our food tour and the need to sell merch that has Tasmania on it.

Tassie green (read: not black). Small logo on the breast, larger design on the back, can be worn front-to-back depending on which way you want to face the world.

## How it's made

Screenprinted by artisanal printers in ink brewed from activated charcoal, rainwater and irony.

## What it costs

A$50 plus a flat A$10 domestic shipping. International shipping is on a case-by-case basis — email **info@daves.com.au** and we'll come back with a quote.

If you're booked on a tour, your guide hands it to you on the day instead of shipping. Mention it in the FareHarbor checkout notes.

## How many

Buy two. You'll wear it, smash a hot pie, get sauce all down your front, and want a clean one for tomorrow.
