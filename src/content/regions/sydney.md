Sydney is where Daves started — one Dave, one pub crawl through The Rocks, eighteen years ago.

## The Rocks

The original tour. Five historic pubs in three hours, starting at the oldest licensed premises in the city. Your guide knows the colonial drinking history cold and the bartenders by name.

## Sydney food tours

Walking food tours through Chinatown, Surry Hills and the Inner West — the same format as Hobart, with a different city's pantry. Yum cha, banh mi, Lebanese sweets, third-wave coffee.

## Day trips

Hunter Valley wine, Blue Mountains lunch and Manly beachside food afternoons all run from Sydney CBD on the Daves small-group format.
