A bespoke private-charter Hobart bucks party tour across the best of southern Tasmania — whisky distilleries, cider houses, breweries and cool-climate wineries — built around the buck, the crew and what you actually want to do with the day.

## Every bucks tour is custom

The venues, the route, the timing, the pace, the lunch spot, the pranks — your call. The list of stops below is what most bucks crews end up doing, but every single bit of it is negotiable. If the buck rates Sullivans Cove over Lark, we'll start there. If you want a 1pm start because the night before was big, we'll start at 1pm. If you want to swap the wineries for an axe-throwing venue, done.

We send a planning email after you enquire, get the brief, and come back with a tailored itinerary and quote inside the same business day.

## What stays the same on every tour

Three things, always:

1. **A sober driver and a private vehicle** for the day — yours, not shared.
2. **No booking fees, no upsells.** The price you're quoted is the price you pay.
3. **Daves goes in front.** Cellar doors, distilleries and pubs get advance notice that a crew is coming in. They pour generously, plate properly, and the people behind the bar are usually the people who made what's in your glass.

## What's typically included

The price covers the vehicle, the driver, all tastings, all entry fees and a sit-down lunch at one of the producers. Dinner is on you (we'll book it if you want a hand). Accommodation is on you. Hangovers are on you.

## How to book

Email or phone with rough dates, group size and the buck's first name. We'll send a planning email and come back with an itinerary and a quote within the same business day.
